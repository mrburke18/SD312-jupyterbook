# Some drills

*Thanks to the best math book I've ever used, Kreyszig's "Advanced Engineering
Mathematics" for these problems.*

$$
\textrm{Let }
A=\left[\begin{array}{cc}
2&1\\
1&7
\end{array}\right],
B=\left[\begin{array}{cc}
-2&5\\
0&8
\end{array}\right],
C=\left[\begin{array}{ccc}
6&0&3\\
1&0&-5
\end{array}\right],
D=\left[\begin{array}{ccc}
4&0&-4\\
-3&4&9
\end{array}\right]
$$

Find the following expressions or give reasons why they are undefined.  You
may (should?) use Python to check your work, but please first do it with
pencil and paper.

1. $A+B$
2. $B+A$
3. $A+B+C$
5. $(C^T)^T$
6. $C+C^T$
7. $5D-3C$
8. $5D^T-3C^T$
9. $(B^T-A^T)^T$
10. $B-A$

$$
\textrm{Let }
a=\left[\begin{array}{c}
1\\4\\3
\end{array}
\right],
B=\left[\begin{array}{cc}
2&-3\\
0&2\\
0&1
\end{array}\right],

4&6&2\\
6&0&3\\
2&3&-1
\end{array}\right],
d=\left[\begin{array}{ccc}
4&3&0
\end{array}\right]
$$

Find the following expressions or give reasons why they are undefined.  You
may use Python to check your work, but please first do it with pencil and
paper.

11. $Ba$
12. $a^TB$
13. $aB$
14. $C^2$
15. $C^TC$
16. $CC^T$
17. $a^Td$
18. $a^Td^T$
19. $da$
20. $ad$
21. $BB^T$
22. $B^TB$
23. $\frac{1}{2}C^2-C$
24. $C(\frac{1}{2}C-I)$
