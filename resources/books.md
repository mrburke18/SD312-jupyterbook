# Policy and Books

[The course policy can be found here](course_policy.pdf).

We will use the below books for required readings:

- (LFD) Abu-Mostafa, Magdon-Ismail, and Lin. __Learning From Data__.
- (ISL) [James, Witten, Hastie, and Tibshirani.  __An Introduction to
  Statistical Learning__](JWHT.pdf). (generously provided for free by the
  authors)
- \(MML\) [Deisenroth, Faisal, and Ong.  __Mathematics for Machine
  Learning__](https://mml-book.github.io/). (generously provided for free by
  the authors)
- \(DL\) [Goodfellow, Bengio, and Courville.  __Deep Learning__](https://www.deeplearningbook.org/). (generously provided for free by the authors)
- \(RL\) [Sutton, and Barto.  __Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction__](http://incompleteideas.net/book/RLbook2020.pdf). (generously provided for free by the authors)
